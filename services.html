Our Services

Comprehensive hearing care designed around you. From initial evaluations to ongoing support, we're here to help you hear better and live better.

Comprehensive Hearing Evaluations

Not sure if you have hearing loss? We'll get to the bottom of it. Our thorough evaluations use the latest diagnostic technology to assess your hearing and identify any issues.

We take the time to understand your unique situation and create a clear path forward. No rushing, no pressure—just honest answers and practical solutions.

  • Complete hearing assessment
  • Advanced diagnostic testing
  • Personalized treatment recommendations
  • Insurance-friendly options

Tinnitus Evaluation & Treatment

That constant ringing, buzzing, or humming can be incredibly frustrating. We get it. Tinnitus affects millions, and while there's no magic cure, there are real solutions that can bring significant relief.

We'll identify what's causing your tinnitus and develop a customized treatment plan using proven therapies that help you manage symptoms and regain your peace of mind.

  • Detailed tinnitus assessment
  • Sound therapy options
  • Lifestyle modification guidance
  • Ongoing support and adjustments

Hearing Aid Fitting & Programming

Finding the right hearing aids is about more than just picking a device off the shelf. We match you with technology that fits your lifestyle, budget, and specific hearing needs.

From the initial fitting to fine-tuning and follow-up care, we make sure your hearing aids work perfectly. And if something needs adjusting? We're always here to help.

  • Personalized device selection
  • Custom fitting and programming
  • Bluetooth and smartphone integration
  • Unlimited follow-up adjustments

Hearing Aid Repairs & Maintenance

Hearing aids are sophisticated devices, and sometimes they need a little TLC. Whether it's routine maintenance or an unexpected issue, we've got you covered.

We offer quick repairs, thorough cleanings, and preventive care to keep your devices running smoothly. Most repairs can be done right here in our office.

  • In-office repairs and cleaning
  • Manufacturer warranty service
  • Battery replacement and accessories
  • Regular maintenance check-ups

Custom Hearing Protection

Prevention is key. Whether you work in loud environments, attend concerts, or enjoy shooting sports, protecting your hearing now can save you from problems down the road.

We create custom-fitted earplugs that provide superior protection while still letting you hear what you need to. Comfortable, effective, and built to last.

  • Custom-molded ear protection
  • Musician's earplugs
  • Industrial hearing protection
  • Swimming and water sports plugs
