Meet Dr. Armin Golian

Dr. Armin Golian brings a passion for helping people reconnect with the sounds that matter most. With years of experience in audiology and a deep commitment to patient care, Dr. Golian has built a practice focused on personalized treatment and cutting-edge technology.

What sets Dr. Golian apart is his ability to really listen. He takes the time to understand each patient's unique challenges and lifestyle needs, creating treatment plans that actually work in the real world. Whether you're dealing with hearing loss, tinnitus, or just want to protect your hearing, Dr. Golian is here to guide you every step of the way.

His approach combines clinical expertise with genuine care. Patients appreciate his straightforward explanations, patience in answering questions, and commitment to finding solutions that fit both their hearing needs and their budget.

Education & Credentials

Doctor of Audiology (Au.D.) Salus University
Bachelor of Science in Speech Pathology California State University, Northridge

Beyond his clinical work, Dr. Golian stays current with the latest advances in hearing technology and treatment methods. He believes that education is just as important as treatment—empowering patients to make informed decisions about their hearing health.

When he's not in the office, Dr. Golian enjoys spending time with family and staying active in the West Los Angeles community. He's proud to serve the area and help people rediscover the joy of clear, effortless hearing.

Our Mission

At Golian Audiology, we believe everyone deserves to hear clearly and live fully. Our mission is to provide exceptional, personalized hearing care using the latest technology and a patient-first approach. We're not just treating hearing loss—we're helping people reconnect with their loved ones, enjoy their favorite activities, and engage with the world around them.
